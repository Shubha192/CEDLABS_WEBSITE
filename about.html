About CED LABS

Who we are: CED Labs Pvt Ltd. is a fast-growing product design, development, and manufacturing company based in Tumkur, Karnataka. Established in 2019, the company was founded by a team of highly experienced engineers with deep expertise in sectors such as Aerospace, Railways, and Telecom. Driven by a commitment to innovation and excellence, CED Labs specializes in cutting-edge technologies, including the Internet of Things (IoT), Radio Frequency Identification (RFID), and embedded systems.

From its inception, CED Labs has aligned itself with India's "Make in India" initiative, aiming to become a leading player in the design and manufacturing of high-quality electronic products. The company's core mission is to deliver end-to-end, customized solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each customer. This customer-centric approach, backed by technical expertise, sets CED Labs apart in the highly competitive technology landscape.
